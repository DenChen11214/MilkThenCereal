This is an interactive pie chart. You can choose the divide sections of the pie chart into different categories based on what you select on the drop down below. Hover over sections of the pie chart to see the number and percentage of games occupied by it. If you click on the section, a list of games will appear. You can click on at most two sections to compare the games contained side by side. You can only select at most 2 sections at a time! To reset the pie chart just click the center.
